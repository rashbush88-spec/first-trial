Who I am

I'm Abdul-Rashid Bushran, a full-stack developer based in Bolgatanga, Ghana — and I'm probably the only developer you'll meet who can build your e-commerce platform and sell you the MacBook to run it on. Through MarshalTech Hub, I've built a cross-service model that's genuinely different: I supply iPhones, MacBooks, and Apple accessories to students, SMBs, and content creators across Ghana — then build the digital infrastructure they need to grow.

On the development side, I ship full-stack systems that solve real business problems: hotel booking platforms, e-commerce stores with Paystack and MoMo integration, property listing sites, POS systems, and management dashboards. I care deeply about performance — most of my clients are on mobile networks, so every kilobyte counts.

The Apple angle isn't just a business line — it shapes how I build. I care about the premium feel, the attention to detail, the sense that software should delight rather than just function. Based in Bolgatanga, serving clients across Accra, Bolgatanga, and beyond.
